#13 · Late Q2 Limit Orders
If a setup is developing late in Q2, place a GTC limit order at your target entry price before halftime. Best prices occur in the final 2 minutes of Q2 as momentum runs hit their maximum — don't wait for the horn.
#15 · 500-Bet Milestone Rule
Do not scale above 3% or commercialize until 500 logged bets. Hold 1/10 Kelly (2.25–3% max) until milestone reached. Current: — / 500.
