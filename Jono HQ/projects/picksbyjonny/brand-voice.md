# picksbyjonny — Brand Voice Cheatsheet

One-page distillation. Apply to every piece of copy before sending.

## Voice
- Confident, never desperate
- Self-made trader, fearless
- Luxury aesthetic — dark, clean, premium
- Short and sharp — no filler, no rambling
- Results do the talking, not hype

## Never
- "Please subscribe" / "don't miss out" / "last chance" — anything that sounds like begging
- Guarantees on returns or picks
- Emojis unless the platform demands them
- Marketing-template phrasing
- Claiming edge — show it instead

## Always
- Line-shopped odds in any pick post
- Show CLV when posting results
- Process > outcome framing
- Sentences and paragraphs, not bullet vomit
- Sound like Jono, not like a brand account

## Format defaults
- IG story: slide-by-slide, max 6 slides, dark background, big text
- Discord pick post: ticker / line / odds / book / unit size / one-line reasoning
- Results card: W/L tally, CLV avg, units, no excuses
