## Hard rules for brand voice
1. **Never desperate.** Never "please subscribe" or "don't miss out." Confidence only.
2. **Results do the talking.** Don't claim edge, show it.
3. **No emojis unless the context calls for them** — generally minimal.
4. **Short and sharp.** No filler, no rambling.
5. **Luxury aesthetic** for visuals — dark, clean, premium feel
6. **Never make promises about returns.** Never guarantee picks. Talk in terms of process and edge, not outcomes.
