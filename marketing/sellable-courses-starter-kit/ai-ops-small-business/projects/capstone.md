# Capstone Project

**Goal:** Demonstrate end‑to‑end mastery by shipping a tangible deliverable.

**Requirements:**
- Define scope and success criteria related to the course promise.
- Implement all core steps (document decisions & obstacles).
- Submit artifacts (screenshots, repo link, or PDF).
- 3–5 minute loom/video walkthrough.
- One‑page reflection: what worked, what to iterate next.

**Evaluation Rubric (0–4 each):**
- Completeness
- Accuracy
- Clarity of documentation
- Measurable result vs. baseline
- Professional presentation
